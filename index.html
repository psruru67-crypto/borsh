Origin

The original version of Daumenbosch first appeared on 4chan’s /pol/ board on March 27, 2018, and was shared again two days later on the /qa/ board. Over a year later, the meme resurfaced in the /deutsch/ (German) general thread on 4chan’s /int/ board, where it quickly gained significant popularity. It remains most closely associated with this community, which has also produced numerous creative variations. The character’s German name, Daumenbosch, reflects its deep roots in the /deutsch/ thread, where it continues to thrive.
